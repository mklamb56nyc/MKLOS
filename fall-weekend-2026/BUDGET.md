## What moves the number
- **Flights are ~60% of it.** SkyMiles award space in Delta One (or Premium
  Select at roughly half the cash fare) is the single biggest lever.
- **Room tier.** Deluxe vs. signature suite is ±€1,500 across 3 nights.
- **Driver tier.** €250 van-and-driver vs. €850 luxury chauffeur for Saturday.

## Verify at booking (in order)
1. Delta One cash + award pricing for Oct 8 out / Oct 12 back (exact October
   schedule publishes with the fare).
2. Penha Longa Oct 9–12 availability — Columbus Day weekend; book refundable.
3. Oitavos tee sheet (their teeone.golf engine) + West Cliffs booking; confirm
   official green fees and Carrie's tee options.
4. Chocapalha private tasting (appointment only — no published price).
5. Saturday driver quote for golf → lunch → quinta → resort loop.
