## Base: Penha Longa Resort (Ritz-Carlton), Sintra
14th-century monastery estate in Sintra-Cascais Natural Park, ~30 min from
LIS. On-site: Atlantic championship course, spa, and the resort's restaurant
collection (incl. both Michelin rooms below).

## Day by day

### Fri Oct 9 — arrive, coast, LAB
- Land ~8am, car or private transfer to Penha Longa (~30 min); drop bags,
  espresso on the estate.
- Afternoon: Cascais — marina, old town, seafood lunch. (Nap window is real;
  don't overschedule.)
- **Dinner: LAB by Sergi Arola** (Michelin ★, at the resort). Open Fri ✓ —
  it's closed Sun–Mon, so Friday is the night for it.

### Sat Oct 10 — Oitavos Dunes + wine
- **Golf: Oitavos Dunes** (~15 min, Quinta da Marinha) — Arthur Hills links
  through dunes and umbrella pines, ocean views. Book the earliest
  comfortable tee time; wind builds in the afternoon.
- Lunch at the Oitavos clubhouse, then a private cellar tour + tasting at a
  Lisboa-region quinta (e.g. Quinta da Chocapalha, ~50 min inland — book
  ahead, tastings are by appointment).
- **Dinner: Midori** (Michelin ★, at the resort) — Japanese × Portuguese
  tasting menu by Pedro Almeida. Open Sat ✓ (also closed Sun–Mon).

### Sun Oct 11 — West Cliffs + unwind
- **Golf: West Cliffs** (Silver Coast, ~50–60 min north near Óbidos) —
  clifftop Cynthia Dye design, routinely ranked Portugal's #1 modern course.
- Afternoon/evening back at the resort: spa, or an extra 9 on the Atlantic
  course, then candlelit petiscos + wine in Cascais (both starred rooms are
  closed Sunday — plan for casual and great).

### Mon Oct 12 — home
- Slow breakfast, check out, drop the car at LIS, late-morning nonstop to
  JFK; home by dinner. Kids' school question (Poly Prep holiday?) is moot —
  we're back Monday afternoon.
