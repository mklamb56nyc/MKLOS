# Bold by Nature — culture & vocabulary layer

Ideas, not yet finalized. This is Phase 6 territory — where Molly's personality
shines. The best programs develop their own internal language (Dartmouth:
"Trips"/"Trippees"/"Croo"; Colby: "on your COOT," cross-COOT meetups, COOT
spelled with bodies on summits; Athenian: "AWE," the "Run-In" through a gate
inscribed *"There's more in you than you think"*; Holderness: "Out Back" and its
3-day solo). Porter's already has **New Girls / Old Girls / Ancients** to build on.

## To develop over time

- Names for the signature experiences
- Names for leadership roles within the program
- A post-outing ritual
- A trail journal (Old Girls passing journals down to New Girls)
- A patch
- Small things that turn a club into an institution

## Borrowable tradition mechanics (from research)

- **Points/ranks for progression** — Storm King's "survival points" with
  JV/Varsity/Captain ranks earned by participation
- **Credentialed independence** — Thacher's A/B-level camper status (earn the
  right to lead your own trips); CRMS's Wilderness Assistant Volunteers
  (returning students selected to help lead orientation)
- **A finishing ritual** — Athenian's Run-In; Dartmouth's arrival at Moosilauke
  Ravine Lodge
- **A physical record** — Colby's decades of tongue-in-cheek trip logs, now in
  the college's Special Collections

## The endgame (north star)

A program so embedded that an **Ancient at her 25th reunion** says *"Remember
that night on the river?"* — and every person in her class knows exactly what
she means.

## Multi-year escalation

- **Year 1:** a small devoted following; build culture; keep the identity
  intentionally "Porter's," not "Outing Club."
- **Years 2–3:** a **"New Girl" outdoor orientation** experience for incoming
  students — the move that turns a club into a community institution (even one
  night camping on the riverbank creates shared class-wide memory).
- **Years 3–5:** a **signature multi-day expedition** for a class year, with its
  own traditions — the version that rivals Athenian/Holderness and gives
  Porter's something no other elite girls' school has.
