====================================================================
- Target Glen Cove (independent city) over the Town of Oyster Bay — controls its own parks/budget.
- Keep the park FREE, public, city-owned. Private board supports; never gates.
- Site follows the underserved population (downtown/south) — serves equity + grant eligibility. Maccarone confirmed LEAD SITE (in a designated DAC, V1.0 + V2.0).
- Morgan Park ruled out as a build site (residents-only); kept for relationships.
- Supervision, if any: volunteer-marshaled and free — never a fee/waiver/residency gate.
- State construction money: MPR (direct city application) was the lead but Round Two is awarded/closed — DASNY via Lavine is PRIMARY until a new MPR/EPF round opens.
- Build-cost working assumption: ~$1.0–1.5M for ~10–12k sq ft (Brentwood-anchored), pending a builder quote.

Lead with (1) PSDG provisioning + national per-capita benchmark, and (2) the ACCESS GAP by bike (car-free/kid) and car; keep 20-min drive as a generous floor.
